## Summary
This paper presents a general synthetic iteration scheme (GSIS) for finding steady-state solutions of the Boltzmann equation in rarefied gas dynamics. The method converges within dozens of iterations across all Knudsen numbers, from near-continuum to highly rarefied regimes. While the paper addresses computational physics (rarefied gas flows, fluid dynamics), it has no direct relevance to memory protection or computer system security.
