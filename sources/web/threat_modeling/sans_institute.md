## Summary
The SANS Institute is a leading cybersecurity training and certification organization that provides expert-led courses across offensive operations, cyber defense, cloud security, digital forensics, incident response, and industrial control systems security. Through GIAC certifications and 85+ courses, SANS trains over 400,000 cybersecurity practitioners globally, serving 159 country governments and 492 Fortune 500 companies. Training spans AI security, penetration testing, SOC operations, and leadership development.

## Key Concepts
- 85+ cybersecurity courses across multiple focus areas
- GIAC certification program for skills validation
- Hands-on cyber ranges and real-world simulations
- AI security training (adversarial AI, AI/ML for defense)
- Focus areas: Offensive Ops, Cyber Defense, Cloud Security, ICS, Forensics
- SANS Cyber Leaders Network for senior executives
- Internet Storm Center (ISC) for threat intelligence
- Free resources: webcasts, whitepapers, posters, tools

## Security Relevance
Provides the training and certification infrastructure for developing cybersecurity expertise needed to conduct and validate threat modeling. The offensive operations courses (penetration testing, red teaming) directly train the skills needed to identify and test system vulnerabilities.
