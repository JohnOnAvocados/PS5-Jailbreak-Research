## Summary
SIE CXD90062GG NVMe SSD controller based on Marvell 88SS1098. Codename: Zao. Controls 12 NAND channels (CH4-CH11) with PCIe Gen4 interface for M.2 SSD expansion.
## Key Concepts
- **Function**: NVMe SSD Controller (Flash Controller)
- **Codename**: Zao
- **Base Design**: Marvell 88SS1098 NVMe SSD controller
- **NAND Channels**: 12 channels (CH4 through CH11) with 8-bit data bus each
- **Channel Signals**: AD[0:7] (data), DQS/DQSB (data strobe), RE/REB (read enable), WE (write enable), CLE (command latch), ALE (address latch), CE0-CE3 (chip enable)
- **PCIe Interface**: M.2 slot with 4 lanes (RC1_0 through RC1_3)
- **Power**: 1.2V_FC_VDDQFIO (NAND I/O), various power/ground planes
- **NAND Interface Voltage**: 1.2V VDDQFIO
## System Role
Internal NVMe SSD controller for PS5 flash storage and M.2 expansion slot interface.
