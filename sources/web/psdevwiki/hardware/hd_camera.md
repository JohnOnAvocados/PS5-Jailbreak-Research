## Summary
PS5 HD Camera (CFI-ZEY1) features dual lenses for 1080p capture and a built-in adjustable stand with background removal tools.
## Key Concepts
- **Product Code**: CFI-ZEY1
- **Resolution**: 1080p full-HD video capture
- **Lenses**: Dual wide-angle lenses
- **Stand**: Built-in adjustable stand, secure positioning above or below TVs
- **Features**: Background removal tools, green screen support, picture-in-picture broadcasting
## System Role
Camera accessory for PS5 enabling video capture, broadcasting, and picture-in-picture gameplay.
