# SKU Models
## Source URL
https://www.psdevwiki.com/ps5/SKU_Models
## System Layer
Hardware / SKU Identification
## Summary
Comprehensive listing of all PS5 retail and non-retail SKU models including chassis revisions, regional variants, limited editions, PS5 Slim, and PS5 Pro models with their release dates, firmware versions, and serial number patterns.
## Key Concepts
- Retail models: CFI-1000 series (first chassis), CFI-1100 series (second chassis), CFI-1200 series (third chassis)
- PS5 Slim: CFI-2000 series
- PS5 Pro: CFI-7000 series (first chassis), CFI-7100 series (second chassis)
- Non-retail: DFI-T1000AA (TestKit), DFI-D1000AA (DevKit)
- Region-specific model endings: A (with disc), B (digital edition)
- Release firmware ranges from 20.01-1.00.00.37 to 24.02-9.05.00.05
- Limited editions: Ratchet & Clank, Horizon Forbidden West, Call of Duty MWII, 30th Anniversary
## System Role
Maps hardware model numbers to their specifications, regions, release dates, and factory firmware versions for identification and compatibility tracking.
