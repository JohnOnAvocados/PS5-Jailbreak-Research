# Title ID

**Source:** https://www.psdevwiki.com/ps5/Title_ID
**System Layer:** Filesystem
**Summary:** Title ID naming conventions and known identifiers for PS5 system applications, internal functions, and retail games.
**Key Concepts:** NPXS, PPSA, ECAS, ELAS, ELJM, Content ID, region prefixes
**System Role:** Standardized application identification system used throughout PS5 firmware and digital distribution.

## Overview

Title IDs are unique identifiers used for both internal system functions/applications and external/sideloaded applications.

## Digital and Physical Title IDs

### ECAS & ELAS (Region: Asia)
Interchangeable IDs used interchangeably:
- ECAS-00006: Sackboy: A Big Adventure
- ECAS-00016: Returnal
- ECAS-00003: Marvel's Spider-Man: Miles Morales
- ELAS-10162: Battlefield 2042
- ELAS-10002: Just Dance 2021
- ELAS-10006: Assassin's Creed Valhalla

### ELJM (Region: Japan)
- ELJM-30022: Godfall
- ELJM-30059: Aliens: Fireteam Elite
- ELJM-30021: Overcooked! All You Can Eat

### PPSA (Region: North America and Europe)
Digital titles only use PPSA regardless of region:
- PPSA-01411: Marvel's Spider-Man: Miles Morales
- PPSA-04609: Elden Ring
- PPSA-04182: Hoa
