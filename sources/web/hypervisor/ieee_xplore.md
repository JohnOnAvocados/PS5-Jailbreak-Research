## Summary
IEEE Xplore is the digital library of the Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers (IEEE), providing access to over 6 million documents including journals, conference proceedings, and standards. It hosts extensive research on hypervisor design, hardware-assisted virtualization, trusted execution environments, side-channel attacks on virtualization layers, and formal verification of hypervisor security properties. Access to full papers requires an institutional or individual subscription.

## Security Relevance
IEEE Xplore is a primary source for peer-reviewed research on hypervisor security, including papers on trusted execution environments, hardware-software co-design for isolation, and side-channel analysis of virtualization layers. It provides foundational literature for understanding the threat model and security properties of hypervisors.
