## Summary
IEEE Xplore is a research database providing access to more than 6 million documents from IEEE journals, conferences, standards, and educational courses. Covers electrical engineering, computer science, and electronics including security, cryptography, trusted computing, and hardware security.

## Key Concepts
- Full-text access to IEEE journals, transactions, conference proceedings, and published standards
- IEEE Security & Privacy Symposium (S&P) is top venue for security research
- IEEE Transactions on Information Forensics and Security, IEEE Transactions on Dependable and Secure Computing
- IEEE standards relevant to security (IEEE 802.1, IEEE 2600 for printer security, IEEE P1363 for cryptography)
- Author analytics, citation tracking, and research trend analysis
- Subscribe and open-access content models

## Security Relevance
Major publication venue for trusted systems and hardware security research, including papers on secure processors, TEE architectures, side-channel attacks, and trusted boot implementations. IEEE standards also define security specifications for networking and embedded systems.
