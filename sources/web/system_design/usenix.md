## Summary
USENIX is a nonprofit computing association focused on systems research and engineering. Organizes top-tier conferences including OSDI (Operating Systems Design and Implementation), USENIX Security (S&P alternative), ATC (Annual Technical Conference), FAST (File and Storage Technologies), and NSDI (Networked Systems). Maintains a no-fee, open-access policy for all published research.

## Key Concepts
- USENIX Security Symposium is one of the top four security conferences worldwide

## Security Relevance
Key venue for trusted systems research including seminal papers on TEEs (SGX, TrustZone), secure boot architectures, hypervisor security, and OS-level trusted computing. Open-access policy ensures broad dissemination of trusted system designs to both researchers and practitioners.
