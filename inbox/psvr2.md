## Summary
PlayStation VR2 headset. Codename: EPCOT (E in EPT-01). Motherboard revision: EPT-01, 1-010-109-11.
## Key Concepts
- **Codename**: EPCOT
- **Motherboard**: EPT-01 (1-010-109-11)
- **Side A Components**:
  - SIE CXD90067GG
  - K4U6E3 4AAMGCL (DRAM)
  - THGBMNG5 D1LBAIT (4GB eMMC/EEPROM)
  - WM1801B 26M7YHS (Onboard Audio?)
  - P9957 03 06 Z2220 (PCA9957 24-channel SPI serial bus)
- **Side B Components**:
  - SIE CXD90068GF
  - RTS5443H M7C88C5 CM34AA (Realtek Type-C Controller/Hub/Card Reader)
- **Power Button Board**: KEY-01, 1-010-112-11
## System Role
Virtual reality headset for PlayStation 5.
