## Summary
The PlayStation 5 is Sony's 9th generation home console, released November 2020. Two models exist: standard (with disc drive) and digital (without disc drive). Largest PlayStation console in size.
## Key Concepts
- 9th generation video game console
- Standard model with Ultra HD Blu-ray disc drive
- Digital model without disc drive
- Released November 12, 2020 in NA/AU/NZ/JP/KR
- Released November 19, 2020 globally
- Released April 11, 2021 in Egypt
