# Media
## Source URL
https://www.psdevwiki.com/ps5/Media
## System Layer
hardware
## Summary
PS5 standard edition includes an Ultra HD Blu-ray drive. Digital edition cannot play discs. Local media playback supports USB drives formatted as exFAT/FAT32. Media playback formats detailed for video (H.264, VP9 up to 4K) and audio (FLAC, MP3, AAC). No CD, 3D Blu-ray, Dolby Vision, or HDR10+ support.
## Key Concepts
- Ultra HD Blu-ray drive (standard model only)
- No disc support on digital edition
- No external USB disc drive support
- No CD playback (any type)
- Blu-ray: region locked, supports Dolby Atmos, DTS:X
- DVD: region locked, 5 region switches
- 4K Blu-ray: region locked, HDR10 and HLG only (no Dolby Vision)
- No 3D Blu-ray support
- USB media: exFAT/FAT32, requires Music/Video/Pictures folders
- Video codecs: H.264 MKV/MP4 up to 4K, VP9 WEBM up to 4K
- No local HEVC playback (disc only)
- Audio: FLAC stereo, MP3, AAC stereo (no DRM files)
## System Role
Optical disc and local media playback
