## Summary
IEEE Xplore is the digital library of the Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers (IEEE), providing access to journals, conference proceedings, standards, and educational content. It contains extensive research on boot chain security, including IEEE S&P (Oakland) proceedings, IEEE Transactions on Information Forensics and Security, IEEE Transactions on Dependable and Secure Computing, and IEEE Security & Privacy Magazine. Key topics include hardware root of trust implementations, secure boot for embedded systems, TPM security analysis, FPGA bitstream encryption, UEFI vulnerability research, and platform firmware resiliency. IEEE standards relevant to boot security are also available.
