## Summary
NIST SP 800-147 (April 2011) provides guidelines for preventing unauthorized modification of BIOS firmware on PC client systems. Covers conventional BIOS, EFI BIOS, and UEFI BIOS stored in system flash memory. Addresses the threat of persistent malware presence through BIOS corruption or implantation.
