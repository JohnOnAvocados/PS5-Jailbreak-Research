## Summary
Official PS5 accessories launched alongside the console in 2020. Product codes: CFI-ZCT1 (DualSense), CFI-ZDS1 (Charging Station), CFI-ZEY1 (HD Camera), CFI-ZMR1 (Media Remote), CFI-ZWD1 (Wireless Adaptor), CFI-ZWH1 (PULSE 3D Wireless Headset), CFI-ZAA1 (PS Camera Adaptor). ## Key Concepts
- **DualSense Wireless Controller**: Bluetooth, USB Type-C, 1,560 mAh battery, 2.402-2.48 GHz, 2.5 mW
- **Charging Station**: Docks two DualSense controllers via bottom charging pins
- **HD Camera**: Dual lenses, 1080p capture, built-in stand, background removal tools
- **Media Remote**: Bluetooth, IR transmitter for TV control, quick access to Disney+, Netflix, YouTube, Spotify
- **PULSE 3D Wireless Headset**: Tempest 3D AudioTech, dual hidden microphones, noise-cancelling, wireless adaptor, 3.5mm jack, up to 12 hours battery
- **PS Camera Adaptor**: AUX (IN), USB 3 Type-A (out), 5V 500mA, dimensions 87x17x25mm, mass ~38g
## System Role
Official accessory ecosystem for the PlayStation 5 console.
