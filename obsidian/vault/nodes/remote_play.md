## Summary
PS5 supports Remote Play for remote access from other devices, including official clients for Android, iOS, macOS, PS4, PS5, and Windows, and the open-source Chiaki client for additional platforms. ## Key Concepts
- Official Remote Play supports: Android, iOS, macOS, PS4 (since FW 8.00), PS5, Windows. - Chiaki (open-source) supports: Android, Linux, macOS, Nintendo Switch, PS5, PS Vita (ported via VitaKi), Windows.
