## Summary
The TPM Library Specification, maintained by the Trusted Computing Group (TCG), defines the standard for Trusted Platform Module (TPM) implementations. The current version is TPM 2.0 Library Specification (revision of November 2019). It is divided into four parts: Part 1 (Architecture), Part 2 (Structures), Part 3 (Commands), and Part 4 (Supporting Routines).
