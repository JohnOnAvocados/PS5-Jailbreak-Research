## Summary
PS5 prototype and development hardware unit naming schemes including devkits (EVA3-3, Prototype 0/1/2), testkits (EGR-TAxxxK), CP boxes (CPB-TAxxxK), controllers (BDT-0250xxx, CFI-ZCT1x), cameras (EDT-001), and media remotes (LPT-D100x, CFI-ZMR1xx). ## Key Concepts
- **Devkits**:
 - EVA3-3: DUTP-DSNxxxBK-Lx (earliest, ~0.75)
 - Prototype 0: DUTP-DSNxxxBK-Rx
 - Prototype 1: DUTP-DSNxxxBK-Wx (e.g., DUTP-DSN18AAK-W5)
 - Prototype 2: DSWxxxBK-xx
- **Testkits** (both are Prototype 1): EGR-TAxxxK-Gx, EGR-TAxxxK-Jx
- **CP Boxes**: CPB-TAxxxK-Bx (min firmware ???), CPB-TAxxxK-Cx (min firmware 0.9.0.5)
- **Controllers**: BDT-0250xxx (Bond), CFI-ZCT1x (may be proto depending on sticker)
- **Cameras**: EDT-001
- **Media Remotes**: LPT-D100x, CFI-ZMR1xx (may be proto depending on sticker)
## System Role
Development and prototype hardware variants of PS5 consoles and accessories.
