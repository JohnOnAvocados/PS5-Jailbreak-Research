## Summary
Comprehensive hardware specifications for PS5 standard and digital editions. Covers CPU, GPU, memory, storage, I/O, wireless, power supply, dimensions, and pricing by region. ## Key Concepts
- CPU: 8-core/16-thread x86-64 AMD Ryzen Zen 2, variable frequency up to 3.5 GHz
- GPU: AMD Radeon RDNA 2, Ray Tracing Acceleration, variable frequency up to 2.23 GHz
- Floating Point Performance: 10.28 TFLOPS
- Memory: 16 GB GDDR6, 256-bit, 448 GB/s bandwidth
- Storage: Custom 825 GB SSD, PCIe 4.0, 5.5 GB/s raw read bandwidth
- External Storage: USB HDD support
- Audio: Tempest 3D Audio Tech
- I/O: Gigabit Ethernet, 3x USB 3.1, 1x USB-C, HDMI 2.1
- Wireless: Wi-Fi IEEE 802.11ax, Bluetooth 5.1
- Video Output: 4K 120Hz, VRR
- Disc Drive: 4K Ultra HD Blu-ray (standard only)
- Power Supply: 350W (standard), 340W (digital)
- Dimensions: 390x260x104mm (standard), 390x260x92mm (digital)
- Weight: 4.5 kg (standard), 3.9 kg (digital)
- Controller: DualSense
