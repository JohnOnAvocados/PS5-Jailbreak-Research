## Summary
The ACM Digital Library is a comprehensive collection of computing and information technology literature published by the Association for Computing Machinery. It includes journals, conference proceedings, magazines, newsletters, and books covering all areas of computer science including security, privacy, architecture, embedded systems, and cryptography. The library features proceedings from major conferences such as CCS (Computer and Communications Security), CODASPY (Data and Application Security and Privacy), and WiSec (Wireless and Mobile Network Security).
