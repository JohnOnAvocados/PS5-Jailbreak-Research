## Summary
IEEE Xplore is a digital library providing access to over 200 journals, transactions, magazines, conference proceedings, and published standards from the Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers. Content covers electrical engineering, computer science, and electronics including significant collections on hardware security, trusted computing, cryptography, embedded systems security, and firmware analysis. IEEE publications include the Symposium on Security and Privacy (S&P), International Symposium on Hardware Oriented Security and Trust (HOST), and IEEE Transactions on Information Forensics and Security.
