## Summary
PS5 Safe Mode is a special boot mode for recovery and maintenance tasks, accessed by holding the power button until two beeps. The DualSense controller must be connected via USB. ## Key Concepts
- **Option 1 - Restart PS5**: Ends Safe Mode and reboots normally.
