## Summary
A Trusted Platform Module (TPM) is a secure cryptoprocessor implementing ISO/IEC 11889 standard. TPM 1.2 was standardized in 2009; TPM 2.0 in 2015 (not backward compatible). TPM 2.0 is required for Windows 11.
