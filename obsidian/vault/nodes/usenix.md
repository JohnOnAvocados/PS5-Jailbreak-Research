## Summary
USENIX is a nonprofit computing organization that hosts conferences including USENIX Security Symposium, USENIX Operating Systems Design and Implementation (OSDI), USENIX Annual Technical Conference (ATC), and WOOT (Workshop on Offensive Technologies). USENIX maintains an open access policy—conference materials and research are free to publish and free to read. Proceedings cover systems security, operating systems, storage, networking, and offensive security research including firmware exploitation, trusted execution environments, and platform security.
