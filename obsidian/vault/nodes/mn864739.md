## Summary
Panasonic MN864739 HDMI retiming chip with DisplayPort input and HDMI output. Codename: FLAVA. ## Key Concepts
- **Function**: HDMI chip with DP to HDMI conversion
- **Codename**: FLAVA
- **Input**: 4-lane DisplayPort (DP0) from SOC
- **Output**: HDMI 2.0 with 4 TMDS channels (D0, D1, D2, CK)
- **Interfaces**: Host I2C (HSDA/HSCL), DDC (SDA/SCL), CEC
- **Power Supplies**: 1.8V, 0.9V analog, 3.3V for various domains
- **Clock**: 27MHz SYSCLK input
- **Key Pins**: HPD (Hot Plug Detect), /HDMI_RESET, /HDMI_IRQ, DPHPD_IRQ (DP Hotplug)
- **Reference**: CL3020 test pad
## System Role
HDMI interface chip for PS5 video output.
