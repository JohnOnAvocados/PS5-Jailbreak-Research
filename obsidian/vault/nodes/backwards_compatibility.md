## Summary
List of PS4 titles tested on PS5 with compatibility ratings: Incompatible, In-Game, Playable, Perfect, Enhanced. The majority of tested titles run at Perfect quality with no issues. ## Key Concepts
- Incompatible: game does NOT boot
- In-Game: game boots but may not be playable
- Playable: game boots with possible minor issues
- Perfect: no perceived issues
- Enhanced: specific PS5 patches/enhancements
- 40+ tested titles documented
- P.T.
