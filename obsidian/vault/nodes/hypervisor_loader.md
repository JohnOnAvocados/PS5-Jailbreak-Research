## Summary
Introduced in FW 03.00.00.33 and above. Codename: "HyLonome". ## Key Concepts
- First appeared in FW 3.00
- Acts as an intermediate loader between the Secure Loader and the Hypervisor
- Codename suggests "Hypervisor Loader — No ME" or similar

## System Role
The Hypervisor Loader adds an additional stage to the PS5 boot chain (introduced in FW 3.00).
