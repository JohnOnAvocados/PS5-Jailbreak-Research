## Summary
Black Hat is a premier information security conference series featuring briefings on cutting-edge security research including firmware exploitation, hardware attacks, boot security, and trusted platform vulnerabilities. The archives contain presentation materials and whitepapers from past conferences covering topics such as UEFI exploits, Intel ME/AMD PSP attacks, TPM bypass techniques, secure boot bypasses, and console jailbreak research. Presentations are categorized by year and region (USA, Europe, Asia).
